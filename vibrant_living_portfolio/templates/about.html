Meet Sean Flanagan

Dedicated to sustainable transformation through evidence-based training and compassionate support.

My Story

My story is much like everybody else's in the fitness industry who is now coaching teams or individuals. From a very young age I fell into different sports — GAA, football, running, golf — before eventually choosing to fully commit to GAA. Being strong on the pitch meant getting strong in the gym, and through that I developed a huge passion for weight training.

I've been training for 8 years now, having gone through multiple cutting, gaining and maintenance phases — paired with an ongoing pursuit to acquire knowledge. I've had an array of different career paths since finishing school in 2020: studying to be a language teacher, then creative media & design, then travelling the world as a full-time content creator before landing in the fitness industry.

Many different paths — but one ongoing theme. Fitness is so much more than aesthetics. It's about how you feel during the process. The opportunity to be of service to others. Sport and fitness has always played a massive role in improving the quality of my life, and now I've built a business that allows other people to have that same experience.
