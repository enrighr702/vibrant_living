My Fitness Journey

My story is much like everybody else’s in the fitness industry who is now coaching teams or individuals. From a very young age I fell into different sports (GAA, football, running, golf) & then I got to a point where I had to focus on one. I chose GAA. Being strong on the pitch meant getting strong in the gym & through that I developed a huge passion for weight training. Over the years, I've given up sport competitively & fully committed to weight training.

I’ve been training for 8 years now & having gone through multiple cutting, gaining & maintenance phases - paired with an ongoing pursuit to acquire knowledge - I think it's fair to say I know what I'm talking about. I’ve had an array of different career paths ever since I finished school in 2020. I studied to be a language teacher, then studied creative media & design, then travelled the world as a full time content creator before landing a job in the fitness industry. I’ve had many different career paths - but I've noticed the one ongoing theme in them all.

Fitness is so much more than aesthetics - it’s about how you feel during the process of it all. The opportunity to be of service to others. Sport & fitness has always played a massive role in improving the quality of my life & now I’ve built a business that allows other people to have that same experience.

Our Philosophy

At Vibrant Living, we are so much more than just a fitness brand. We specialize in personal training designed to help you move your body, feel your best, and achieve your goals. But we know that wellness involves much more than just working out. It’s about nourishing your body with good food, connecting with others, and enjoying yourself along the way.

We’re here to help you make health & fitness part of your everyday life, in a way that’s enjoyable, sustainable, and fulfilling. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming and supportive space where you can thrive - physically, mentally, and socially.
